Paper Society

Project Overview

Paper Society is an online store that offers high-quality paper and stationery goods from diaries and notebooks to wedding invitation or business card templates. The core belief of the brand is that paper is timeless. It guides us for a long time and stays with us in the future. The brand creates inspiring and unique stationery design that makes the customers proud to use them. The aim of this task was to design an online store, that helps users find products quickly and easily to match their particular needs. This project has been created as part of my UI Design programme at CareerFoundry.

Time Frame

The app has been created within 5 weeks.

My Role

UX/UI designer, included creating user flow, wireframing, prototyping, user testing, mockups

Tools

  • Adobe XD
  • Photoshop
  • Illustrator
  • Flowmap

Problem Statement

Users don’t have the time or ability to visit physical stores and would rather buy their goods on the go or from home. They also struggle to find good-quality and functional stationery in traditional stores.

Solution

Create an online store that allows users to purchase desired stationery and have them delivered to any address. The inventory can be filtered in particular ways that meets the best the users’ needs. As it is a responsive website users can shop on any device whether on mobile or on desktop and from anywhere, where they can connect to the internet.

Wireframes

With the essential funcionalities in mind I started to sketch the screens both for mobile and desktop version. After several sketches I picked the best ones and developed them into a mid-fidelity wireframe in Adobe XD.
