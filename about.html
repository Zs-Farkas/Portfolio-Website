About me

My Story

I am a user interface designer with a decade of experience in desktop publishing. I have been part of a variety of print projects from the beginning to creating print-ready files. In the past I worked at different companies, which taught me how to adapt quickly and successfully to different company cultures and processes. I gradually shifted towards digital design, which finally led me to UI design. What really excites me in this field is that it focuses on real user and merges aesthetic and functionality. My goal is to create valuable and beautiful products.
